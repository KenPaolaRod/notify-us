Team collaboration app that simplifies communication

Reach your team across multiple communication channels. All right from Slack.

Create your topic

A topic is a subject or theme that a user can subscribe to in order to receive updates or notifications.



Share your topic

It's important to let others know about your topic. Send a Slack message encouraging them to subscribe.

Create subscriptions

Subscribers can receive notifications sent for a topic. These notifications can be received through Slack, email, or external systems by using webhooks.



Send a test

It's important to make sure that the subscription has been configured correctly.

Send notification

Topic owners will publish messages to their topics. Subscribers will receive the message near real-time if it matches their selected notification types.
